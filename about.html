Hi there!

I'm Ollio, Product Designer. Throughout my career, I've had the opportunity to explore and work across different disciplines, bringing together insight, strategy, imagination, technology, and craftsmanship in my creative process. Collaboration is key for me, as I love teaming up with inspiring individuals, innovators, and brands. Together, we aim to create work that looks to the future, carries meaning, resonates with communities, and has the power to drive positive change. It's a fulfilling journey, and I'm always eager to take on new challenges.

About me

Studied Digital (Design) at (Kyiv Projector Institute). And have been working in the design industry for over a (6) years.

My passion for design started when I was young, and I have pursued this passion by working with a variety of clients and projects over the years. It is important for me to create a meaningful design and carefully study it, to create a design that will be effective.
